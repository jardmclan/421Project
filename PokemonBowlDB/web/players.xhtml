This tool allows you to find information on players in a variety of categories.

Type in the player's Discord username, their friend code, or their In-Game-Name (IGN) to search for a particular player. Then select from the dropdown menu what specific category of information you wish to know about that player. Categories: General gives information on the player's Discord name and what games they play; Events gives information on their tournament performance; Teams retrieves data on what Pokemon teams they have registered; Breeding generates a list of all the Pokemon they have bred or are breeding, as well as how many of each Pokemon they have.
