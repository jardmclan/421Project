What is the Poke(mon) Bowl Discord DB?

The Poke(mon) Bowl Discord DB is a database set up by members of our very own Discord chat server, with the intent of helping our members in all their Pokemon related pursuits. In particular, it stores info on trading and breeding activity of the Discord's members, competitive and casual events held by the group, useful info about each member and their teams, as well as useful resources for player improvement.
